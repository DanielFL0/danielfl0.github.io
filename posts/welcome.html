When is it going to end

April 26, 2022

Today I received what one of the most unexpected messages I've ever gotten. Not only was it unexpected I also believe it was unprofessional for reasons I'll explain later in this same document.

The conversation went like the following:

  • HR: "Hey Daniel, good afternoon"
  • HR: "It's HR from $company_you_work_for"
  • HR: "Do you have a minute?"
  • Me: "Yeah!"
  • HR: "Well it turns out your interniship contract is ending soon, you might want to check on that"
  • HR: "If you're interested you can renew your contract by talking with your college department"
  • Me: "Sure, when does my contract end?"
  • HR: "This friday"

Excuse me, what the fuck?

Tell me if I'm wrong, but the general consensus is that if I want to quit a job I must AT LEAST send a 1 week notice before my leave date, why is it ok for HR to tell me that my contract is ENDING 3 DAYS BEFORE?
